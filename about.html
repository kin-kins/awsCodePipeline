About Us.

Meet our talented team.

Once there existed an amazingly talented developer Bhala. Bhala lived in the ruins of ramapuram and coded furiously day-and-night. People from lands unknown approached Bhala for seeking knowledge and expertise in technology, cause he wasn't an ordinary coder he was what we call a true developer. Be it front end, back end or graphic design, he had mastered it all; since no man was able to do such marvel before in the humble village of ramapuram thus he became the the one and only developer people could count on. And thus started referring to him as Bhala the developer. As time grew bhala the developer wanted to pass on this legacy to geeks who saw him as a legand. He decided to do so by creating this site. So if you are here...consider yourself blessed!
